The Unity Day

Schedule :

9:00 - 11:00 / Art Workshops

Paiting activity for aboriginal and neo-australians children to meet each other.

11:30 - 13:00 / Cricket Match

In every major cities, a cricket game will take place with aboriginal and white australians on both teams.

13:00 / Barbies

People gather around barbecues to learn each other's origins and culture.

18:00 - 20:00 / Parade

This will be the most significant time of the day for the country.

20:00 - 23:00 / Opera Ballet

The opera will offer and broadcast a special ballet retracing the history of Australia.

23:00 / Fireworks

The pyrotechnic show will take place in most of the cities all around the country.
